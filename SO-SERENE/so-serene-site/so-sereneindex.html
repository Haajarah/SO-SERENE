So Serene

Smell as good as you look

Serene is Oudh based scents company that was started in 2015 by a mother of six who back then was also working full time as a childcare worker. The company started in the UK but now has stockists in Kenya, Tanzania and Oman too. In England we sell our products in London, Manchester and Milton Keynes as well as Edinburgh in Scotland.
